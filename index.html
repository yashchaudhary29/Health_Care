Lekin kya aapne kabhi socha hai ki Elon Musk ya bade leaders ke paas bhi 24 hi ghante hote hain? Farq sirf Time Management ka hai.

Is article mein hum un 10 practical tips ki baat karenge jo busy logon ke liye kisi vardaan se kam nahi hain.

1. "Eat That Frog" Strategy (Sabse Mushkil Kaam Pehle)

Hamari aadat hoti hai ki hum aasaan kaamo ko pehle karte hain aur mushkil kaam ko taltey rehte hain. Brian Tracy kehte hain ki din ka sabse bada aur mushkil kaam (The Frog) sabse pehle khatam karein.

Fayda: Ek baar bada kaam ho gaya, to baaki din tension-free nikalta hai.

2. Time Blocking Ka Use Karein

Sirf To-Do list mat banayein, apne calendar mein waqt block karein.

Kaise karein? Maano subah 10 se 11 baje tak sirf "Emails" check karne hain, to us waqt aur kuch mat kijiye. Isse focus bana rehta hai.

3. Pomodoro Technique (25:5 Rule)

Agar aapko focus karne mein dikkat hoti hai, to ye technique best hai.

25 minute kaam karein.

5 minute ka break lein.

Ye cycle 4 baar karne ke baad ek bada break (20-30 min) lein.

4. Notifications Ka Jaal Todie

Research kehti hai ki ek baar phone check karne ke baad dimaag ko wapas kaam par lagne mein 23 minute lagte hain.

Tip: Kaam ke waqt WhatsApp aur Instagram ke notifications off rakhein.

5. "No" Kehna Seekhein

Har kisi ki madad karna acchi baat hai, lekin apne waqt ki keemat par nahi. Agar aap pehle se busy hain, to naye kaam ko polite tareeke se 'No' kahein.

6. 80/20 Rule (Pareto Principle)

Aapke 20% kaam aise hote hain jo aapko 80% results dete hain. Pehchaniye ki wo kaunse zaroori kaam hain aur apna zyada waqt unhi par lagayein. Faltu ki meetings aur lambi baaton se bachein.

7. Agle Din Ki Taiyari Raat Mein Hi Karein

Busy logon ki sabse badi galti hoti hai subah uthkar sochna ki "Aaj kya karna hai?"

Raat ko sone se pehle agle din ke Top 3 Tasks likh lein. Isse aapka agla din structured guzrega.

8. Delegation (Kaam Baantna Seekhein)

Aap superhero nahi hain jo sab kuch khud karenge. Jo kaam koi aur kar sakta hai (jaise ghar ki safayi, grocery laana, ya data entry), use outsource karein ya kisi aur ko dein.

9. Multitasking Se Bachein

Ek saath do kaam karne se kaam jaldi nahi hota, balki galti hone ke chances badh jaate hain. Ek samay par sirf Ek hi Kaam (Single-tasking) karein.

10. Self-Care Ko Time Dein

Agar aapki machine (body aur mind) thak jayegi, to productivity zero ho jayegi.

Roz 7-8 ghante ki neend lein.

15 minute meditation ya exercise zaroori hai.

Conclusion

Time management ka matlab ye nahi hai ki aap har waqt kaam hi karte rahein, balki iska matlab hai ki aap apne waqt ko itne acche se manage karein ki aapko apne shauq (hobbies) aur family ke liye bhi waqt mil sake. Aaj hi inmein se koi bhi 2 tips chunein aur shuruat karein!
